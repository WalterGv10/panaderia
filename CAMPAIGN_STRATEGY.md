# Campaign Strategy: Guatemalan Bread "The Hamptons Edition"

## 1. Value Extraction (Deep Dive)
- **Target Audience:** Guatemalan diaspora in The Hamptons, lovers of artisanal bread, residents of The Hamptons looking for authentic international flavors.
- **Killer Feature:** "Authentic Heritage + Home Delivery". The fusion of traditional Guatemalan recipes with the convenience of delivery in an exclusive area like The Hamptons.
- **Tone:** Premium, Nostalgic, Trustworthy.

## 2. Marketing Angles

| Angle | Hook/Concept | Copy Snippet |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Logical (Efficiency)** | Freshness & Convenience | "Horneado hoy, en tu mesa hoy. Delivery directo en The Hamptons sin salir de casa." |
| **Emotional (Nostalgia)** | A Taste of Home | "¿Extrañas el olor del pan francés recién salido del horno? Traemos Guatemala a tu puerta." |
| **Social (Authority)** | The Local Favorite | "Mynor Veliz: El panadero artesanal que está conquistando The Hamptons con sabor auténtico." |
